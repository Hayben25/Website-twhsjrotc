Meet The Instructors!

Lieutenant Colonel (Retired) Chris McMartin

Chief Master Sergeant (Retired) Clark Gilleo

Chief Master Sergeant (Retired) Roger Garcia

Senior Aerospace Science Instructor LtCol McMartin, US Air Force Academy Class of 1995, started at The Woodlands in 2015 after 20 years active duty as a C-141 and C-130 navigator, and an Assistant Professor at the Army Command and General Staff College at Fort Leavenworth. He is an instrument-rated private pilot and scuba divemaster.

Aerospace Science Instructor Chief Gilleo started at The Woodlands in 2021 after 30 years of service split between the Air National Guard, active duty Air Force, and Air Force Reserve. He has experience as a Military Training Instructor, a Recruiter (including regional management and Superintendent roles), and engineering with a RED HORSE unit.

Aerospace Science Instructor Chief Garcia started at The Woodlands in 2023 after 24 years active duty as a Communications Operator and Signals Intelligence Analyst, and 10 years as a JROTC instructor at Forney High School, Texas.
